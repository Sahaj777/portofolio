FAQ

Common questions.

Anything else? Send me a message.

What industries do you work with?
My core focus areas are finance & CA firms (invoicing, GST, compliance automation), banking & lending (document processing, credit analysis), SaaS products (AI feature development), and enterprise companies needing Salesforce or CRM AI integrations. I also work with ecommerce brands and general SMBs automating repetitive knowledge work.
How long does a typical project take?
A focused AI automation module (e.g. invoice OCR pipeline) takes 3–4 weeks. A full AI SaaS product build runs 8–14 weeks. Enterprise integrations and agentic systems fall between 4–8 weeks depending on API complexity. I'll give you a precise timeline during scoping.
Do you work with open-source models or only GPT/Claude?
Both. For most enterprise clients, GPT-4 or Claude is the right choice due to reliability and capability. For clients with data privacy requirements or cost constraints, I build with Llama 3, Mistral, or other open-source models hosted on your own infrastructure. Model selection is always ROI-driven, not brand-driven.
What does your engagement look like post-launch?
Every project includes a 60-day support window for bug fixes and minor adjustments. After that, I offer optional retainer arrangements for ongoing development, monitoring, and feature iteration. Many clients work with me on a retainer basis after the initial build.
What's your pricing structure?
I work on project-based fixed pricing for well-scoped builds, and time-and-material for exploratory or evolving projects. Discovery calls are always free. Pricing is shared after the scoping session, when I have a clear picture of what needs to be built. Projects typically range from ₹1.5L for focused automation modules to ₹8L+ for full-stack AI products.
Is my data safe when you build AI systems?
Data security is built into the architecture from day one. I can design systems that keep sensitive data fully within your cloud environment. For regulated industries (banking, CA), I implement data masking, encryption at rest and in transit, audit logging, and PII handling that meets compliance standards. NDAs are standard practice.
